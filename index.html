About HYKY

HYKY is a WebXR experience viewer that allows you to explore immersive 3D and augmented reality content directly in your browser.

Use the navigation arrows to cycle through different WebXR experiences. Each experience will load in fullscreen mode for optimal immersion.

WebXR requires a compatible browser and device. For AR experiences, you'll need a smartphone with AR capabilities. For VR experiences, you may need a VR headset.

HYKY is designed to showcase the potential of WebXR technology and provide easy access to high-quality immersive experiences.
